With the baseball season now in full swing, the NBA playoffs underway and the Stanley Cup playoffs right around the corner, it is an exciting time for sports.  And for sports APIs.  In the past few months, there have been a number of exciting events that have brought sports data and sports APIs to center stage.Sports are the perfect use case for APIs.  In a sports game, leads can change every minute, big plays can happen in a second and an injury can end a team’s dream of a championship in an instant.
