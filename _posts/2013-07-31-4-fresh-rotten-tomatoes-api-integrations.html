By opening up their API to the public, Rotten Tomatoes has seen a sharp increases in business partnerships and audience growth.Prior to the release of their public API, Rotten Tomatoes was only able to develop one new partnership at a time. A lot of time and resources was dedicated to integrating their Tomatometer scores and aggregation of movies reviews into partners’ products.Rotten Tomatoes public API has made it easy for partners and developers to create new products that utilize Rotten Tomatoes’ features. Since Q1 of 2012, its traffic has increased 378% quarter-over-quarter.
